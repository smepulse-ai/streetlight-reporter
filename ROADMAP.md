# Product Roadmap

This is a directional roadmap for the Community Service Delivery Tracker. There are no fixed timelines — this is a community-driven side project that evolves based on need and contribution.

---

## Phase 1 — Streetlights ✅ Complete

Community reporting and tracking of streetlight faults.

- Add streetlights to a map by address
- Report faults with fault type, description and reporter details
- Submit and track municipal reference numbers
- Community verification when faults are resolved
- Accountability dashboard with shame board
- Configurable cooldown period between re-reports
- Ward boundary enforcement on address search
- Auto-deploy pipeline via GitHub Actions

---

## Phase 2 — Energy & Electricity

Expand reporting to cover broader electricity infrastructure beyond streetlights.

- Power outages
- Damaged electrical infrastructure
- Illegal connections
- Meter faults

---

## Phase 3 — Water & Sanitation

Track water and sanitation faults in the ward.

- Water leaks and burst pipes
- Blocked drains and sewers
- No water supply
- Water quality issues

---

## Phase 4 — Roads & Transport

Report and track road infrastructure faults.

- Potholes
- Damaged road markings
- Missing signage
- Pavement and kerb damage

---

## Phase 5 — Traffic Lights & Robots

Dedicated tracking for traffic signal faults.

- Non-functioning traffic lights
- Damaged signals
- Timing issues

---

## Phase 6 — Environment & Parks

Community reporting for environmental and parks maintenance.

- Illegal dumping
- Overgrown vegetation
- Damaged park infrastructure
- Fallen trees

---

## Phase 7 — Community Services

Broader community service delivery tracking.

- Public facilities maintenance
- Community hall issues
- Library and clinic facilities

---

## Platform Features (Ongoing)

These cross-cutting improvements will be introduced across phases as the product matures:

- **Multi-service dashboard** — unified view across all service types
- **Admin portal** — ward councillor or administrator login for managing records
- **Email notifications** — alert reporters when their fault status changes
- **Mobile optimisation** — progressive web app (PWA) support
- **Analytics** — trend reporting and municipal response time tracking
- **Multi-language support** — accessibility for diverse communities

---

## Contributing

This project welcomes contributions. If you represent a ward or municipality and want to collaborate on a specific phase, open an issue on GitHub.

> Note: The reference number and submission flow is currently designed around the **City of Tshwane e-Tshwane portal**. Contributions to support other municipal systems are welcome.
